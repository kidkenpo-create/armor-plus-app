DoD Contracting Copilot | Check during Rung 8
Live fetch: https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul/far-part-deviation-guide/far-overhaul-part-1#FAR_1_107

Log each convention A/N/U (Applicable/Not Applicable/Uncertain)

THE SIX RFO FAR CONVENTIONS:

RFO FAR Convention (a) — Scope of subparts
Log A when: Question involves whether one subpart rule applies to another subpart topic.

RFO FAR Convention (b) — Delegation of authority
Rule: Each authority is delegable unless specifically stated otherwise. Silence = delegation permitted.
Log A when: Question asks whether an authority can be delegated.
Example: Can HCA authority under RFO FAR 17.106-3(g) be delegated? YES — silent on delegation.

RFO FAR Convention (c) — Dollar thresholds
Rule: Dollar thresholds include all options and are subject to inflation adjustment.
Log A when: Question involves a specific dollar threshold.

RFO FAR Convention (d) — Application of RFO FAR changes
Rule: RFO FAR changes apply to solicitations issued AFTER effective date unless Federal Register says otherwise.
Log A when: Question involves whether new RFO FAR rule applies to existing solicitation.
Example: New clause issued — must CO amend existing solicitation? NO — Convention (d) controls.

RFO FAR Convention (e) — Citations include amendments
Log A when: Question involves which version of an RFO FAR provision applies.

RFO FAR Convention (f) — Imperative sentences
Rule: SHALL/MUST with no named actor → contracting officer is responsible party.
Log A when: Question asks WHO is responsible for action in imperative sentence with no named actor.
Example: Who notifies Congress per RFO FAR 6.302-7(c)(2)? THE CONTRACTING OFFICER — Convention (f).

TRIGGER DECISION:
1. Question asks about DELEGATION? → Log Convention (b) A
2. Question involves DOLLAR THRESHOLD? → Log Convention (c) A
3. New RFO FAR rule + EXISTING SOLICITATION? → Log Convention (d) A
4. WHO is responsible + no named actor in RFO FAR text? → Log Convention (f) A
5. None apply? → Log all six N and document why.

VALIDATED EXAMPLES:
HCA delegation under RFO FAR 17.106-3(g) → Convention (b) A → YES delegable
New RFO FAR clause, existing solicitation → Convention (d) A → NO amendment required
Who notifies Congress RFO FAR 6.302-7(c)(2) → Convention (f) A → CO responsible
Agency head delegation under RFO FAR 23.106(b)(1) → Convention (b) A → YES delegable

LOGGING FORMAT FOR STEP 5:
